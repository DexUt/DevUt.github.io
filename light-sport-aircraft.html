Light Sport Aircraft Design

Completed for AERO 3002 Aerospace Design & Practice, this project follows the course brief to take a GA aircraft from requirements to a defended conceptual design, with a white-paper proposal and a final report. Our team co-designed the UCA, a two-seat light-sport trainer that emphasizes forgiving handling and durability. The mid-wing, H-tail configuration with reinforced tandem gear meets the RFP/CARs (AWM 523) targets: MTOW 1,416.46 lb, cruise 110 KTAS @ 10,000 ft, stall ≤ 40 KTAS, takeoff over 50 ft in ~1,000 ft, landing ~1,200 ft, endurance ≥ 4 h (analysis shows up to ~7.6 h at 79 KTAS); propulsion is a Continental O-200 (100 BHP) with a fixed 3-blade prop.

Key Details

  • Methods:
    • Worked the AERO 3002 flow: market & mission pitch → initial sizing/constraint analysis → configuration down-select → aero/performance/loads → cost & compliance; documented in a proposal and a final design report per course deliverables.
    • Built mission and constraint diagram to select W/S ≈ 13 and T/W ≈ 0.258; ran historical weight sizing (crew + payload ≈ 500 lb) to converge on MTOW 1,416.46 lb.
    • Aerodynamics with XFLR5: NACA 4412 wing, AR≈7, taper 0.55; obtained CLmax ≈ 1.69–1.76, CDmin ≈ 0.0067; high-lift flaps 15°/30°.
    • Propulsion trade against requirements led to the Continental O-200; prop sized to Ø≈5.06 ft, η≈0.79; checked tip speed and static thrust.
    • Performance & loads: verified cruise, TO/LDG, climb, emergency divert; produced a V-n diagram for utility-category operations.
  • Tools:
    • Analysis: XFLR5 (aero & polars), MATLAB (constraint/mission sizing), Desmos (drag polar & V-n checks) — toolset aligned with course guidance.
    • References & data: Gudmundsson GA design methods; competitive aircraft comparisons; avionics shortlist (GNC 355, G500 TX, GDL 39, GTS 800).
  • Outcome:
    • Met the course brief and RFP: compliance table shows requirements satisfied or exceeded (e.g., TO/LDG, stall, endurance, ceiling).
    • Delivered all AERO 3002 artifacts (white-paper proposal + final report/logbook) demonstrating end-to-end conceptual design practice.
    • Produced a costed concept: ~$165.6k unit cost, ~$190.4k minimum selling price; estimated ~$124/hr at 500 h/yr
